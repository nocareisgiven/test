STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. It represents an approach to education and careers that integrates these four fields.STEM education aims to prepare individuals with the skills needed to thrive in a technology-driven and rapidly changing world. STEM careers cover a wide range of professions, including scientists, engineers, computer programmers, healthcare professionals, researchers, and more. The integration of these fields encourages holistic problem-solving and innovation, contributing to advancements in various industries and driving societal progress.

Women have rocked STEM!

Imagine STEM as a big puzzle made up of science, technology, engineering, and math. Women are like the special pieces that make the puzzle complete and even more exciting.Women have made important contributions in STEM.They've discovered new things, invented cool gadgets, built amazing structures, and solved tricky problems.
